Three Doors of Fate

    Tech Stack: Godot, Aseprite

    Game made for Brackeys Game Jam 2024.1

    Three Doors of Fate is an engaging quiz game where you're tasked with answering 10 questions centered around a specific theme. The game offers you a selection of hints hidden behind doors, allowing you to choose the one that resonates most with you. Your challenge is to identify the correct image concealed by a black silhouette within a 15-second timeframe. The quicker your response, the higher your score. Aim to achieve the highest score possible. The game is designed with replayability in mind, providing the option to select different hints, which means you can enjoy at least three unique playthroughs with varying questions each time. This setup encourages you to keep playing and beating your previous scores.
